### 对客户

1. 先发 `产品设计说明`
2. 再发 `产品手册`

   - `docs/PROJECT_QUICK_GUIDE_2026-03-27.md`

2. 再看 `docs/PROJECT_QUICK_GUIDE_2026-03-27.md`
